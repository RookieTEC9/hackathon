Meet Swift -- the co-founder and CEO of Major League Hacking, the world's official student hackathon league. He has made it his personal mission to enable hackers by helping them get the tools and resources they need to turn their crazy ideas into reality. Swift previously founded Hacker League (acquired by Intel in 2013) and was formerly a developer evangelist for SendGrid. Swift is also widely known for his champion craw-walking abilities and for his mastery of the board game Clue.
